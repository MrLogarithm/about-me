Bootstrapping

  • Does not require much training data
  • Interpretable: can see which context features contribute to a particular labeling
  • Easy-to-understand procedure resembles human process of building up a body of evidence, from obvious cases to more subtle ones

In Sum

  • There is rich information in the numeric parts of ancient accounting corpora, although most research to date focuses purely on text.

  • Study of numerals allows us to quantify subjective intuitions, achieve more nuanced understandings of sign use and relationships.

  • Cross-corpus parallels demonstrate commonalities across disparate accounting traditions, begin to clarify relation of proto-Elamite to proto-cuneiform.

  • Enabled by automated processing of digitized corpus: faster, more precise, easier to visualize results than manual approaches.

  • Enables expert analysis without aiming to replace it.
