Tools & Software

Writing Well is Hard

Visualization suite designed to assist with academic editing by allowing you to compare and contrast different versions of a manuscript. Developed in collaboration with Letitia Henville and the rest of the Writing Well is Hard team.

GSoc 2020: CDLI Accounting Corpora

Analysis and visualizations for Sumerian accounting texts, completed with the Cuneiform Digital Library Initiative (CDLI) as part of Google Summer of Code 2020.

pe-decipher-toolkit

Companion to Born et al. 2019. Includes our cleaned dataset and code samples which replicate the published results.

Proto-Elamite and Proto-Cuneiform n-Gram Viewers

Interface for exploring n-gram frequencies in proto-Elamite and proto-cuneiform. The raw backend data is available in the pe-pc-datasets repository.
