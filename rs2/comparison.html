This table summarizes the proto-Elamite syllabic sign inventories proposed in Meriggi 1971 (La scrittura proto-Elamica) and Dahl 2019 (Tablettes et fragments proto-élamites / Proto-Elamite tablets and fragments). Signs are listed as "shared" if and only if the given sign number occurs identically in both lists. Variants are not considered identical: for example, where Meriggi includes M057 and Dahl includes M057~a, we do not consider this to constitute an overlap between the two lists. Some of the sign numbers given by Meriggi are no longer used in the transliterations of the texts. No attempt has been made to match these signs up with their possible equivalents in the modern system of transliteration.

** Meriggi lists sign no. 62, which is not among the proposed syllabic signs in Dahl 2019. However, the associated image depicts sign 32, which is present in both lists.
